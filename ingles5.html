Cast

Jacob Elordi

The young dreamer who sails along the Seine and becomes instantly enchanted by a mysterious woman.

Simone Ashley

The elegant and magnetic woman who awakens in the protagonist a passion almost impossible to control.

Michael B. Jordan

The mysterious man who interrupts the enchantment and reveals a bitter vision of love.

Bruna Marquezine

The woman from Michael B. Jordan’s past, a symbol of idealized passion and disillusionment.

SCENE 01

Spring on the Seine

Jacob Elordi calmly sails along the Seine River on a golden spring afternoon. The wind dances across the water like liquid silk, and everything seems wrapped in an almost unreal romance.

SCENE 02

The Gaze

In another boat, Simone Ashley appears wearing light-colored clothes that reflect the sunlight. Jacob becomes completely hypnotized by her gaze. The silence between them feels more intense than any conversation.

SCENE 03

The Interruption

Before Jacob can follow the woman, Michael B. Jordan appears unexpectedly. His face carries the weight of someone who has already known a devastating love. He says that spring is dangerous for distracted hearts.

SCENE 04

The Past

Michael reveals that he too fell in love during the spring. The woman was Bruna Marquezine. He believed he had found a perfect love and surrendered himself completely to the feeling.

SCENE 05

Disillusionment

After the marriage, the idealized passion slowly faded away. Michael realized that he had fallen in love with the fantasy created by spring, and not with reality.

SCENE 06

The Last Boat

When Simone Ashley disappears into the distance, Jacob tries to follow her. Michael holds him back, like someone stopping another person from walking into an invisible fire. The river remains calm, but spring is still dangerous.

“In The Spring”

Based on the short story by Guy de Maupassant.
A delicate romance wrapped in mist, desire, and disillusionment. A cinematic storyboard inspired by the short story by Guy de Maupassant. Between silent rivers, sudden passions, and romantic illusions, spring blossoms like a dangerous character.
